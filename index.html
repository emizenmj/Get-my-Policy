Planning Ahead

In a way, health insurance is a lot like car insurance. But, unlike car insurance, health insurance covers far more than just the costs of a bad accident. Health insurance often covers things like annual checkups, vaccinations, preventive health, and other “routine maintenance” for your body and mind. It’s as if car insurance helped pay for oil changes and tire rotations. Therefore it is wise to do planning for your affordable health insurance in advance.
